CELEBIPLUG

CelebiPlug is a 60-second cinematic recorder. Drop a GeoJSON, pick up to two POIs, hit record — and walk away with a Mapbox 3D satellite + terrain + buildings WebM, composed and captured straight from your browser. No FFmpeg, no backend.
